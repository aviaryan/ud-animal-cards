Dragon

Some dragons can spew fire from their mouth.

  • Grouping: Mythology
  • Sub Grouping: Mythological hybrids
  • Habitat: Mountains, Seas, Skies
  • Found: In many cultures worldwide

A dragon is a legendary creature, typically scaled or fire-spewing and with serpentine, reptilian or avian traits, that features in the myths of many cultures around world. The two most well-known cultural traditions of dragon are the European dragon and the Chinese dragon.
